So fragrant! So colorful! So beautiful!

Roses belong in every garden. They provide elegant blooms throughout the summer and are a longtime favorite for fresh floral arrangements.

If you are concerned that roses are difficult to grow, put your worries aside. Today's roses are disease and insect resistant. Discover the joy and ease of growing these modern, carefree roses.

Fragrant roses are the perfect addition to any sunny summer garden. Placed along a walkway, you'll want to linger awhile as their heady perfume and peaceful beauty envelop you.

Plant Habit or Use: Small to medium size shrub
Exposure: Full Sun
Flower Color: Red, Pink, White, Yellow, Orange, Rust, Purple
Blooming Period: Summer-Fall
Height: 24 in. - 3 ft
Width: 24 in - 3 ft.
Plant Character: Perennial shrub
Water Requirements: Moist, well-drained

Uses: Beds, Borders, Hedge, Landscapes, Ornamental, Outdoor
